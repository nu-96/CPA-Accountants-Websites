# SmallBiz CPA Template

A friendly, conversion-focused template for CPA firms targeting small businesses.

**Tier:** Mid ($1,000-$1,500)  
**Type:** Multi-page with pricing focus

## Pages Included

1. **index.html** - Conversion-focused homepage
2. **services.html** - Detailed service descriptions
3. **pricing.html** - Transparent pricing with tiers
4. **resources.html** - Free downloads and blog
5. **contact.html** - Lead capture form

## Features

- ✅ Announcement bar for promotions
- ✅ Trust indicators and ratings
- ✅ Pricing table with tiers
- ✅ Add-on pricing section
- ✅ FAQ section
- ✅ Free resource downloads
- ✅ Newsletter signup
- ✅ Detailed lead capture form
- ✅ Mobile responsive

## Unique Elements

- Fixed monthly pricing display
- Transaction-based tier calculator
- Service checklists
- Social proof (ratings, reviews)
- Low-friction CTA language
