Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a transaction?

A transaction is any entry on your bank or credit card statement—deposits, withdrawals, payments, transfers, etc.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect the following month.

Is there a contract?

Nope! All plans are month-to-month. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

What software do you use?

We work with QuickBooks Online and Xero. If you don't have accounting software yet, we'll help you set it up.
